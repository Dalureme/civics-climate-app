Why I Care

Why Others Should Care

  • Health: smoke and heat harm lungs and hearts.
  • Money: disasters and food shocks raise costs.
  • Fairness: those who pollute least often suffer most.
  • Future: clean energy creates safer jobs.

What You Can Do

Do Now

  • Walk, bike, or bus short trips.
  • Turn off unused electronics.
  • Eat one plant-based meal per week.

At School

  • Start a compost/recycling challenge.
  • Propose a low-waste lunch week.
  • Plant trees or garden.

Community

  • Join a cleanup or tree planting.
  • Write city council about green energy.
  • Support thrift shops and local farmers.
